Combined Resistors

The spaceship's crew is in serious trouble now since the electronic circuit board for warp-speed-engine-control has blown and they're stalled in outer space.

One resistor on the printed cicuit board has turned into smoking carbon but luckily someone found in the service docs what value it has to replace it with.

And then, after hours of searching the box with spare parts, the horrified conclusion is that the requested resistor is not there !!!

However maybe . . . will they be able to make a combination of resistors as a replacement ?
